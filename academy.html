Bootcamps don't work

If you're reading this you want to become a programmer. Your choices are:

  • Learn by yourself - requires mad discipline
  • Join a code bootcamp - over $10k for a short program with questionable results
  • Get a computer science degree - more than $10k for several years and most of the learning is impractical
  • Join the Arcanium Academy as an apprentice!

Apprenticeships work pretty well

If you want to become an electrician, you're not going to go to a bootcamp. You're not going to learn it by yourself (you might explode). And there are no four year degrees. You have to become an apprentice. Apprenticeships are one of the oldest forms of training. You work, you learn, and you get paid. Makes sense to me.

The Arcanium Academy

The Arcanium Academy is a 1-2 year apprenticeship program for web developers. At the Arcanium Academy you will receive a well rounded education that will deeply prepare you for the software development industry. You will also get your hands dirty on real life development tasks, open source contributions and real life projects. Rather than treat you like a child, you will be part of our team. Your program will be indiviualized to your interests and skill level. I assure you, anyone with dedication can become a professional programmer.

Curriculum

Here are some general things that most students will cover. Once again, it's up to you. But I recommend this stuff:

UNIX Philosophy and Command Line

  • Text editing with Vim or Emacs
  • How Linux Works
  • Docker
  • Git
  • Zshell
  • Package managers
  • BASH scripts
  • Makefiles
  • UNIX philosophy
  • Understanding hacking, UNIX, Linux & Open Source

Modern Frontend Web Development

  • modern HTML/HTML5
  • modern CSS
  • ES7+ Javascript
  • React.js
  • Redux pattern
  • Gulp, Webpack & other build tools

Modern Backend and Infrastructure Development

  • Building APIs with Node.js
  • Microservices vs monoliths
  • Databases - MongoDB, PostgreSQL
  • Cache - Redis, Memcached
  • Specialized tools: ZeroMQ, ETCD
  • Deployment - Docker, Kubernetes, Rancher
  • Cloud platforms - Heroku, AWS, Google Cloud, Digital Ocean
  • Testing and continuous integration

Earn while you Learn

After you've learned the basics, we will do our best to secure funding for you. This might involve finding a local internship or working as a freelancer for our agency. We know this is a really important point.

Required Reading

Building your library is essential. From the get-go, we will recommend that you read at least 1 chapter of a code related book per day. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Hackers and painters
  • Data intensive systems
  • Design Patterns - Gang of four
  • Code Complete
  • The Pragmatic Programmer
  • Secrets of the Javascript Ninja

Leaving the Nest

Many of our graduates work for Arcanium.io. Beyond that, we will do our best to reach out to our networks to find you a job. With the skills you will learn, you will have no trouble finding a well paying job. Well paying remote work is very attainable.

Location and Meetups

Arcanium is a remote-first, digital nomad organization. That means we work from everywhere. If you want to learn in person, we can schedule a meetup.

The Academy currently has members from Florida, Connecticut, Seattle, Toronto and Denver. We love the great outdoors and traveling, so we are always planning fun vacations for our members. If you like the great outdoors, travel and coding, you'll fit right in!

(yes we code from ski resorts.)
