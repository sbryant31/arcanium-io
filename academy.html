Bootcamp

The Arcanium Academy

Most bootcamps don't work. They cram too much material into too short of a time period. There's not enough one-on-one teaching.

At the Arcanium Academy you will receive a well rounded education that will have future employers excited to hire you. We have a thorough focus on proper software design, open source, startups, UNIX philosophy and modern web development. We provide individualized, one-on-one mentorship.

We believe it shouldn't break your bank to learn software, so we provide internships to our full time students to earn-while-you-learn.

We built the Arcanium Academy because we have a passion for changing lifes. We truly want to help you change your career and change your life. If you are willing to put in the hard work, we will have your back and personally commit to helping you succeed.

Internship

As an apprentice, you'll begin to put your theory into practice, working on real world code projects. This will include both open source publications and potentially, an internship. During this time you will be continually mentored by a senior mage. By the end of this arc, you should be prepared for an entry level software development job.

Job Placement

Once an adept has worked on several projects in industry and contributed meaningfully, they should naturally progress towards helping other mages. They should also find themselves increasingly able to take on higher level projects. As the scope of a mage's work grows, they will progress to Journeyman. Though they have graduated the Arcanium, it is expected that they will remain a member to share their increasing knowledge with junior wizards.

Tuition

Tuition is $500/month. Many of our students have been prepared for an entry level job in as few as three months. Internships are available to earn while you learn.
