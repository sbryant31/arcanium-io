Apprenticeship

The Arcanium Academy

Most bootcamps are too expensive and too short. Getting a computer science degree takes four years and costs an arm and a leg. So we decided to try something different.

The Arcanium Academy is a 1-2 year apprenticeship program for web developers. At the Arcanium Academy you will receive a well rounded education that will have future employers begging to hire you. You will also get your hands dirty on real life development tasks, open source contribnutions and launching startup products.. We provide individualized, one-on-one mentorship. Heck, we've even been known to offer a place to stay while you learn.

We built the Arcanium Academy because we have a passion for changing lives. We know how intimidating becoming a software developer is. With our community and mentorship, you will always know that we have your back. I guarantee it. So what will you learn?

Internship

As an apprentice, you'll begin to put your theory into practice, working on real world code projects. This will include both open source publications and potentially, an internship. During this time you will be continually mentored by a senior mage. By the end of this arc, you should be prepared for an entry level software development job.

Job Referrals and Placement

We also run a software Agency that many of our graduates work in. Beyond that, we will do our best to reach out to our networks to find you a job. With the skills you will learn, you will have no trouble finding a well paying job. Well paying remote work is very attainable.

Meetups

The Academy currently has members from Florida, Connecticut, Seattle, Toronto and Denver. We love the great outdoors and traveling, so we are always planning fun vacations for our members. If you like outdoor sports, hiking, camping, and coding, you'll fit right in!

(yes, we code from cabins in the mountains sometimes.)

Cost of the Program

The full time program costs $500/month. After the first 3 months, we start our interns at $10/hr USD, so you will have the ability to earn enough to live while studying with us.
