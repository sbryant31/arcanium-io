The Arcanium Academy

The Arcanium Academy is a 1-2 year apprenticeship program for web developers. At the Arcanium Academy you will receive a well rounded education that will deeply prepare you for the software development industry.

You will learn a flexible, in-demand javascript development stack. You're going to be building real life projects as part of a team. And you will have the opportunity to earn money while mastering your skills.

Internships

Honestly, we're not huge fans of practice projects. We've found that the best way to learn something is to need to learn it to solve a problem.

We'll do our best to find you interesting, real life challenges. We can also guide you through the startup process to launch your own products! This isn't just a capstone project, real life work is the core of our curriculum..

Let's talk about remote work.

We are 100% remote

Arcanium is a remote-first, digital nomad organization. That means we work from everywhere and anywhere.

The Academy currently has members from Florida, Connecticut, Seattle, Toronto and Denver.

We are adventurous, passionate people who believe that you can live your dream life and still make an honest living. Through your time at the Arcanium, you will learn the ins and outs of being a remote software developer.
