How much will it cost?

Tuition

All the information you need to become a programmer is already available on the internet. We don't think this career change should cost you an arm or a leg. At the same time, we've learned from experience that you won't value your education unless you have skin in the game. So we will work with your situation. We want to provide an option for everyone.

The program is generally 3-12 months, including a 6-12 month internship.

  • Option A: $500/month
  • Option B: $100/month + $10k deferred tuition (pay after you get a job)
  • Option C: 0 monthly fee + 20% of post-grad earnings for 2 years
